    POLITICAL SYSTEM OF HIMACHAL PRADESH

    THE POLITICAL SYSTEM OF HIMACHAL PRADESH IS NOT VERY COMPLEX TO UNDERSTAND.BECAUSE THERE ARE NOT MANY POLITICAL PARTIES IN THE STATE .THERE ARE CONSISTENTLY TWO DOMINATING PARTIES ONE IS BHARTIYA JANTA PARTY (BJP) AND SECOND IS INDIAN NATIONAL CONGRESS(INC).THE SOME OTHRE PARTIES SUCH AS AAM AADMI PARTY(AAP),BAHUJAN SAMAJ PARTY(BSP) AND MANY MORE ARE STILL STRUGGLING TO MAKE GOVERNMENT IN HIMACHAL PRADESH. IF WE TALK ABOUT SEATS THERE ARE 68 SEATS IN THE LEGISLATIVE ASSEMBLY , 4 SEATS IN THE PARLIAMENT , AND ALSO 4 SEATS IN THE RAJAY SABHA IN THE STATE.

    CURRENT RULING PARTY IN THE STATE

    CURRENTELY,THERE IS INDIAN NATUONAL CONGRESS (INC) IS RULING IN THE STATE . A NATIONAL CONGRESS PARTY(NCP) WON THE LEGISLATIVE ASSEMBLY ELECTION IN DECEMBER 2022.
